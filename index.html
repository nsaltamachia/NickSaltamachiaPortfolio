About me

I am, if nothing else, a storyteller. Always have been...and I've been telling them through the lens of a camera for over a decade.

Throughout my career I've earned increasingly higher-responsibility roles and developed increasingly efficient and engaging ways to succeed in them. And, after mastering the production and post-production elements, I began to focus on the marketing strategies that give these stories purpose. Now, I operate and consult on the entire process from strategy and concept to production and publication.

I truly enjoy creative messaging strategy and production. It's why I'm here. I put great value in the trust that's placed in me to communicate on behalf of a brand or individual. I'm always looking for new stories to tell, and to help others tell theirs.

Services

In my 12 years as a creative professional, I've developed a lot of skills.

Cinematography

I've filmed and directed photography on hundereds of projects with a combined focus on storytelling, style, and efficiency. I can use any DSLR, mirrorless, or cinema camera system, and any camera support, lighting, and grip equipment.

Post-Production

I've used Adobe Creative Suite for 12 years on videos, photos, graphics, and animations while delivering strategic, platform-specific content. I've also used Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Avid Media Composer.

Marketing

As an agent of the brand, I've developed and executed dozens of marketing strategies and campaigns with SMART objectives and KPIs. I believe in the power of a well-told story to create and maintain brand loyalty and awareness.

Photography

I've done a little bit of everything on photography side, too...from products for e-commerce to family and professional portraits to corporate and private event coverage.

Graphic Design

As much as a video or photo can leave an impression, so can a logo, a font, or a color scheme. I love to create visual identities that are unique and memorable while guiding the viewer with design principles like proportion, emphasis and hierarchy.

Web Design

I recently completed a ~500 hour software engineering immersive with General Assembly. I love to create clean, simple, and intuitive user experiences. I'm a big fan of the "less is more" approach, and I'm always looking for ways to make the mundane more exciting.
